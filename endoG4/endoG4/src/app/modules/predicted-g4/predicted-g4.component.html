Predicted quadruplex sequence (PQS) based on genome sequence

It allows sequence searches that take into account possible divergence from the optimal G4 base composition. The advantage of the prediction was the creation and training (parameterisation) of an advanced scoring model, which resulted in increased accuracy compared to similar tools.
